Animal

About

Animal is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language action thriller film co-written, edited and directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga and produced by T-Series and Cine1 Studios .The film stars Ranbir Kapoor, Anil Kapoor, Bobby Deol, Rashmika Mandanna and Tripti Dimri. The cinematography has been handled by Amit Roy and the production design by Suresh Selvarajan. Animal was scheduled to be released on 11 August 2023, but was pushed to 1 December 2023 due to pending post-production work.
