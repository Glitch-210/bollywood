Ganpath

About

Ganapath: A Hero is Born is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language science fiction action film directed by Vikas Bahl, who produced the film under his banner Good. Co in association with Jackky Bhagnani, Vashu Bhagnani and Deepshikha Deshmukh under Pooja Entertainment. It stars Tiger Shroff in the title role, alongside Amitabh Bachchan and Kriti Sanon. The film was announced in November 2020 and underwent a long, heavy pre-production stage. Principal photography commenced in November 2021 and wrapped up in February 2023 with filming taking place in United Kingdom, Ladakh and Mumbai. It is reported to have a budget of ₹200 crore. Ganapath was initially planned to release in December 2022, but was postponed. It is now scheduled for theatrical release on 20 October 2023, coinciding with Dussehra.
